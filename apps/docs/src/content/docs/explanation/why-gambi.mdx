# Why Gambi?

**Gambi** is the short form of **gambiarra**.

We renamed the project because the shorter name works better across the surfaces people touch every day:

- it is easier to say and remember in English
- it fits better in CLI commands like `gambi serve`
- it produces cleaner package names like `gambi` and `gambi-sdk`
- it keeps the original Brazilian identity of the project instead of replacing it with something generic

## What does gambiarra mean?

In Brazilian Portuguese, **gambiarra** does **not** have to mean sloppy work.

The meaning we want here is closer to:

- creative improvisation under constraints
- resourcefulness when you do not have ideal conditions
- making useful systems with what is available
- solving real problems with community spirit and technical cleverness

That is the spirit of this project.

Gambi is local-first, practical, portable, and a bit scrappy in the best possible sense. It helps people share local LLM infrastructure without waiting for a perfect centralized platform.

## The short version

- **Official name:** `Gambi`
- **Origin:** abbreviation of `gambiarra`
- **English positioning:** shorter, clearer, easier to adopt
- **PT-BR meaning:** ingenious workaround, improvisation with care, creativity under pressure
